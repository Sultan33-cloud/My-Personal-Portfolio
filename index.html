Childhood Memories

Early Years in Mombasa

Born and raised in Mombasa, Kenya, I spent my early years surrounded by a loving family and vibrant community. My earliest memories are filled with the sounds of Mombasa's bustling streets and the warmth of Kenyan sunshine.

Family Traditions

Growing up in a traditional Kenyan household, I learned the importance of family, respect, and community. Weekly family gatherings and holiday celebrations were central to my upbringing, teaching me the value of connection.

School Days

My primary school years at Ebenezer Academy were formative. I discovered my love for learning, made lifelong friends, and participated in school events that built my confidence and social skills.

Exploring Nature

Family trips to Diani, Nairobi, and the Kenyan upcountry side exposed me to the breathtaking beauty of my country. These experiences fostered my appreciation for nature and conservation.

Educational Journey

Primary Education (2012-2019)

Attended Ebenezer Academy in Bondo where I developed foundational skills in mathematics, sciences, and languages. Served as class prefect in my final year, learning early leadership skills.

High School (2020-2023)

Completed my secondary education at Sawagongo School, specializing in sciences. Participated in the computer club and science congress team, winning several competitions in both areas.

University (2024-Present)

Currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering at Kirinyaga University. Active in several tech communities and private project innovations to apply classroom knowledge to real-world problems.

Extracurricular Activities

Beyond academics, I've been involved in community service programs, university tech seminars, tech friend group creation, and innovative adventures. These experiences have taught me teamwork, discipline, and time management.
